4th Annual Bloomington Wrestling Golf Scramble

This event will be held Saturday, August 25th at Highland Golf Course in Bloomington, Illinois. The format will be a 4-man, best ball scramble. Low team score will determine the event winners.

Registration begins at 7:30am, with tee-offs beginning at 8:00am. Please show up 30 minutes early. You will be contacted with an exact tee time. The cost for this event is a $60 donation per person ($240 for a 4-man team). and includes entry for four players into the tournament, 18 holes of golf, riding cart, two free drink tickets, a Bloomington Wrestling t-shirt, lunch-on-the-grill for all and free wings and appetizers buffet after golfing at the Main Street Grill in Bloomington.
